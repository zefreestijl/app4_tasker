N.2. Team Selection Procedure

How will you select future team members in regards to the above delivery strategy and contract type?

Selection Criteria Checklist

  • BIM Capability Assessment: Review of past projects and LOD delivery to ensure alignment with project goals.
  • Software Proficiency: Verification of specific software skills (e.g., Revit, Navisworks, Civil 3D) required for the project.
  • Collaborative Experience: History of working in a shared/federated model environment (CDE experience).
  • Resource Availability: Capacity to staff the project with qualified BIM personnel for the duration.
  • IT Infrastructure: Ability to meet hardware and network requirements for seamless collaboration.
