Purpose

This documentation serves as a comprehensive record and defines the formal process for submitting, reviewing, approving, and authorizing BIM models for use in the project.

It ensures that all models are quality-assured, meet project information requirements, and are authorized for distribution following the Plannerly model lifecycle workflow: In Progress ➔ Shared ➔ Published (and Archived).
