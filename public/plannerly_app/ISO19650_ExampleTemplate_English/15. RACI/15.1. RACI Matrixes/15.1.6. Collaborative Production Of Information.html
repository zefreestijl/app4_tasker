Collaborative Production Of Information

This phase represents the core of the BIM execution process. It focuses on the coordinated generation of data by task teams and the rigorous quality control gates required before information is shared within the Common Data Environment (CDE).

TASK APPOINTING PARTY THIRD PARTY LEAD APPOINTED PARTY APPOINTED PARTY
Check availability of reference information and shared resources C A R
Generate information I R/A
Undertake quality assurance check C I R/A
Review information and approve for sharing C R/A
Information model review I C R/A I

Quality Gate Protocol: Every Appointed Party is Accountable for their own internal QA. The Lead Appointed Party provides the final coordination review (Information Model Review) before the data is presented to the client.
