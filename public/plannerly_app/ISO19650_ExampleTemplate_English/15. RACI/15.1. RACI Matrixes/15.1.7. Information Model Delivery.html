Information Model Delivery

The Information Model Delivery stage focuses on the formal handover of coordinated data. This involves a two-step verification process: first, authorization by the Lead Appointed Party, followed by final acceptance by the Appointing Party (Client).

TASK APPOINTING PARTY THIRD PARTY LEAD APPOINTED PARTY APPOINTED PARTY
Submit information model for lead appointed party authorization I A R
Review and authorize the information model I C R/A I
Submit information model for appointing party acceptance A R
Review and accept the information model R/A C I

Authorization vs. Acceptance: Authorization is a technical confirmation by the Lead Appointed Party that the model is coordinated. Acceptance is the Appointing Party's confirmation that the model meets the business and project objectives defined in the EIR.
