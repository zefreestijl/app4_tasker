Tender Response

The Tender Response stage shifts the focus to the prospective delivery team. It is the period where the Lead Appointed Party demonstrates their strategy, capability, and capacity to meet the Appointing Party's information requirements.

TASK APPOINTING PARTY THIRD PARTY LEAD APPOINTED PARTY APPOINTED PARTY
Nominate individuals to undertake the information management function I A R
Establish the delivery team's (pre-appointment) BIM execution plan C R/A C
Assess each task team capability and capacity A R
Establish the delivery team's capability and capacity I R/A
Establish the delivery team’s mobilization plan R/A C
Establish the delivery team’s risk register I R/A C
Compile the delivery team's tender response I R/A

Pre-Appointment Tip: The Pre-appointment BEP is a high-level response to the EIR. The Lead Appointed Party must ensure all Appointed Parties (sub-consultants/contractors) have confirmed their ability to deliver before finalizing the response.
