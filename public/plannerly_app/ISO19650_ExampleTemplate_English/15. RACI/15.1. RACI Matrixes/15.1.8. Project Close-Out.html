Project Close-Out

The Project Close-Out stage ensures that the Information Model is preserved as a legal and operational record and that the institutional knowledge gained during the project is captured to benefit future delivery teams.

Asset Handover: Archiving the project information model (PIM) is a precursor to the Asset Information Model (AIM). The Appointing Party must ensure the archive is secured in a location that supports long-term accessibility for facility management.
