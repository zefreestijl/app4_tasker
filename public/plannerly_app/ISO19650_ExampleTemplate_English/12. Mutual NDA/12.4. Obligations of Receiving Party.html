Obligations of Receiving Party

A receiving party shall:

  • Non-Disclosure: Not disclose the Confidential Information to any third parties without the prior written consent of the disclosing party.
  • Limited Use: Use the Confidential Information only for the purpose of evaluating or engaging in the potential business relationship.
  • Protection of Secrecy: Take all reasonable steps to protect the secrecy of and avoid disclosure or use of the Confidential Information to prevent it from falling into the public domain or the possession of unauthorized persons.
