Sheet Requirements per Level

This schema aligns the three implementation levels (Lite, Intermediate, and Full) with the various project work stages. It serves as a definitive guide for identifying which COBie worksheets must be completed to satisfy specific information requirements at each milestone.

Figure: COBie worksheet completion matrix across implementation tiers.

Implementation Tip: Not all tabs are required for every project. By selecting the appropriate Level at the start of the project (documented in your BEP), you can significantly reduce the data entry burden on the design and construction teams.

Source: Nogueira, R. (2022), "From BIM to Asset Management - Data Driven Guidelines for Operations and Maintenance", University of Minho.
