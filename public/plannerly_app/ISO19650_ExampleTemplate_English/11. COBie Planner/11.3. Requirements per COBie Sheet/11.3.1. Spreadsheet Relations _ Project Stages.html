Spreadsheet Relations & Project Stages

The COBie data structure is dynamic, with different sheets gaining prominence as the project lifecycle advances. Understanding these relationships is key to maintaining data integrity from initial design through to facility occupancy.

Design Stage

Focus on Spatial Properties. The Facility is organized via Zones and Systems. Floors contain Spaces, which serve as the containers for future Components.

Construction Stage

Focus on Equipment Definitions. Technical specifications are solidified into Types, and individual physical instances are logged as Components.

Handover & In-Use

Focus on Operational Tasks. Maintenance is managed through Jobs, which link to the Resources and Spares required for completion.

Figure: Relational map showing how COBie data sheets interconnect throughout the project lifecycle.

Common elements (such as Contacts and Attributes) are cross-cutting; they provide additional context and are updated continuously through every stage of the project.
