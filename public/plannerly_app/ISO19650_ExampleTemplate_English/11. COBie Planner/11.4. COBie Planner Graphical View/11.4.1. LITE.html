COBie Implementation: LITE

The LITE level represents the baseline for COBie data delivery. It focuses primarily on spatial organization and identifying the responsible parties for the project's digital information. This level is essential for establishing the structural foundation of the Asset Management database.

Figure: LITE level data requirements, emphasizing spatial hierarchy and contact information.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Contacts: Complete list of stakeholders responsible for data entry.
  • Spatial Hierarchy: Accurate mapping of Facilities, Floors, and Spaces.
  • Zones: Functional grouping of spaces (e.g., HVAC zones, Fire zones).

Source: Nogueira, R. (2022), "From BIM to Asset Management - Data Driven Guidelines for Operations and Maintenance", University of Minho.
