What is COBie

Possible Exchange Formats

COBie does not invent or define a new technology for data exchange. Instead, it relies on existing industry-standard formats that can be read by multiple familiar software applications, such as Microsoft Excel:

  • IFC STEP (.ifc)
  • XML (.xml)
  • SpreadsheetML (Excel compatible)

COBie is a standardized way to deliver information and is the open-source approach to collect usable Facilities Management (FM) data. The goal is to organize data developed and collected during the project to provide facility owners and operators with structured data.
