Invitation To Tender Information

All Appointed Parties shall read, understand and agree to the requirements of the EIR as it is cascaded down through the whole project supply chain.

The EIR is submitted for tender as part of the project information requirements and is required to be agreed and accepted as the Appointing Party's Exchange Information Requirements (EIR) in the terms of engagement to the contract.

Our goal, in contracting to this EIR and associated resources, is to ensure that:

  • We comply with international standards.
  • Only information associated with a strategic purpose is produced.

Included in this Invitation To Tender

  • Exchange Information Requirements (EIR)
  • Tender response requirements and evaluation criteria
  • Project information delivery milestones
  • Project’s information production methods and procedure
  • Reference information and shared resources (within the project’s common data environment)
  • Asset Information Requirements (AIR)
  • Responsibility Matrix
  • Project’s information protocol
