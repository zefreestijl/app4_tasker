CDE Requirements

We will establish a Common Data Environment (CDE) to support the collaborative production and sharing of information on this project. We may appoint a third party to host, manage or support the CDE.

The CDE will be in place prior to issuing the invitation to tender with this EIR.

CDE REQUIREMENT DETAILS
All information containers will have a unique ID The unique ID shall be agreed and documented along with the fields separated by a delimiter.
All Information Containers will have the following attributes assigned: Status (suitability), Revision and Classification.
Information containers to transition between states Work In Progress, Shared and Published.
User and date of transition between each state Record of when the state changed (from Work In Progress to Shared) and who made the change.
Access restrictions at an information container level Control over who has access to each Information Container.
