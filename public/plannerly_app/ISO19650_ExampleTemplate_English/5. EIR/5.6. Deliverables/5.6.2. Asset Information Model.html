Asset Information Model

The Asset Information Requirements are detailed in the Scope.

Data Transfer Requirements

The Lead Appointed Party, operator, operations team or facility manager should confirm that all project information and data for operational purposes have been transferred from the Project Information Model (PIM) to the Asset Information Model (AIM).

Action Required: Where this is not the case, the design and construction team should take action to ensure there is no further delay in transferring the required information and data.
