Operations Activities

Design & Construction Team

The team should prepare for operation of the facility, including the initial and extended periods of aftercare, based on the following:

  1. Conduct aftercare review meetings and workshops
  2. Record user comments related to functionality and effectiveness
  3. Maintain records of site walks to detect emerging issues
  4. Fine-tune the building services engineering systems
  5. Fine-tune the structural monitoring and control systems
  6. Record and feedback details of all fine-tuning
  7. Update the Asset Information Model (AIM)
  8. Update the facility handbook
  9. Update the facility user guide

Lead Appointed Party & Operator

The Lead Appointed Party, operator, operations team or facility manager should prepare for operation of the asset/facility, including the initial and extended periods of aftercare, based on the following activities:

  1. Record and review early energy usage for comparison with predictions
  2. Review and record Building Management System (BMS) monitoring of environmental conditions to detect any emerging problems
  3. Set up a helpdesk with a physical presence
  4. Prepare and circulate newsletters or utilize other media for communicating directly with end-users
