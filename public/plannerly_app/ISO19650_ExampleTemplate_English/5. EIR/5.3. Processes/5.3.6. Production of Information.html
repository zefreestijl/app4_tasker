Production of Information

The following information should be considered to support the work activities and contribute to the deliverables:

  1. Project information and design information needed to enable construction
  2. Alignment of the design with the needs of the operator, operations team or facility manager, as appropriate, and end-users in terms of the basis of the design, space, access, adaptability and operational cost, as a minimum
  3. Availability of a procedure or protocol for controlling the distribution of documents, information and data
  4. Requirements to obtain firm price quotations as a precursor to procuring the building services engineering systems and other long-lead items
  5. Updated lead times for building services engineering systems and other major components and systems
  6. Source of specialist maintenance service provision (e.g. lifts/elevators and other specialist building services engineering systems)
  7. Definition and extent of operation and maintenance information
  8. Method statements for work where existing building services engineering systems and public utilities are to interface with the new or refurbished asset/facility
