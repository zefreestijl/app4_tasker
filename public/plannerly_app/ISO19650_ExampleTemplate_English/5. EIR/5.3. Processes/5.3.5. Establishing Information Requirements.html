Establishing Information Requirements

All teams will apply the Level of Information Need (EN 17412) approach using the Scope module.

Our goal is to ensure that specific and achievable requirements are defined - we seek to be purposeful and specific when it comes to information requirements.

Defining the Requirements

Each purpose (why) will be described for an Information Delivery Milestone along with start/finish dates (when) and a list of all objects (what) that are required.

The Level of Information Need (how) will be captured in the Scope grid with:

  1. Geometrical Information (Shape, size, location)
  2. Alphanumerical Information (Properties, data, attributes)
  3. Documentation Requirements (Manuals, reports, certifications)
