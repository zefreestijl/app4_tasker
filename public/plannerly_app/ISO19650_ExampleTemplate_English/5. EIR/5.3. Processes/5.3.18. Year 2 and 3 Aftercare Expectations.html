Year 2 and 3 Aftercare Expectations

At the end of the extended period of aftercare in Years 2 and 3, the operator, operations team or the facility manager should execute the following ongoing activities:

Annual Review Requirements

  1. Continue its performance evaluation.
  2. Prepare the annual reports on performance with an explanation of improvements, changes, and good or bad performance.
  3. Prepare reports on the performance of materials and equipment.
  4. At the end of each year, feedback the findings of performance evaluation to the design and construction team and the Appointing Party's Information Manager.
  5. Retain data on measured performance to inform the benchmarking of required outcomes and performance targets for future projects.
  6. Request the attendance of senior representatives (design & construction team) for a workshop with the Appointing Party and end-users.
  7. Report to the Appointing Party on performance during the year, the actions considered necessary to optimize the asset/facility, and the lessons learned.
