Equipments and Fixtures

The quantification methods for equipment and fixtures can vary according to the available information:

  • If the items are modeled:

    They should be quantified by type and specification. The measurement unit may vary based on the nature of the element:

    • Count (Each): Pumps, valves, drains, etc.
    • Length (m or ft): Gutters and other linear elements.
  • If the items are not modeled:
    They should be included in a total sum, clearly described and specified within the corresponding Bill of Quantities (BoQ) item.
