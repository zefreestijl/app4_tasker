Main Structural Elements

When quantifying steel structures through BIM, accurately calculating the weight of the main structure requires identifying the length of the steel profiles.

Critical Rule: For this calculation, it is important to rely on the geometrical length (e.g., cut length in Revit), rather than relying on the axis length.

Structural authoring software typically generates analytical elements for structural analysis. However, to ensure accurate steel material quantification, it's crucial to utilize the geometric dimensions of these elements to avoid duplicate quantities.
