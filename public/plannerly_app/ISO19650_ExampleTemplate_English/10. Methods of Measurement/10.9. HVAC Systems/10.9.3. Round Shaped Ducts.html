Round Shaped Ducts

These elements should be measured by length (meters/feet), diameter, and material.

Quantification Logic

  • Early Design Stages: Fittings should be measured by length.
  • Advanced Project Phases: A quantification per unit (count) is preferable for fittings.

In both scenarios, quantities must be segregated by diameter and material to ensure pricing accuracy.

Figure 17 - Illustration of the quantified length of round-shaped ducts.

Special Case (Insulation/Fire Protection): It is necessary to consider these as a distinct typology from regular ducts. Any specific characteristics in the composition should result in a new element type/class.
