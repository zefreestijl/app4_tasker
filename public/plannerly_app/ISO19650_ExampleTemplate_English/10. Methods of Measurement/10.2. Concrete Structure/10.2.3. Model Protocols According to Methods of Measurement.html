Model Protocols According to Methods of Measurement

This approach involves modeling the structure considering the guidelines of the rules of measurement to be considered on the project.

Note: In some cases, those are not aligned with the default behavior of authoring tools and demand adjustments on each element, which can be extra time-consuming.

Nonetheless, it offers clear advantages over the 2D approach, considering that not only the results remain consistent with rules of measurement, but also leverages the full benefits of a BIM project.
