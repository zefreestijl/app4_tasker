Modelling Add-ins

Customized add-ins within the authoring software involve selecting structural elements from the model and defining rules to model formwork geometry and extract quantities in the schedule.

This method offers more precise quantities by considering not only element intersections but also specific features like inclination and openings. Moreover, it ensures traceability of the modeled quantities.

Limitation: However, its use is limited when applied to complex geometry like stairs and "model in-place" elements.
