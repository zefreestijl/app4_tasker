Asset Lifecycle Milestones for Information Delivery

Not all information is needed at the same time or for the same reason. In this section, we define the Delivery milestones that drive the need for asset-related information throughout the asset lifecycle.

Purpose of Milestones

These milestones help us clarify:

  • When we expect certain data to be delivered
  • What that data is used for
  • How it supports our long-term asset management goals

ISO 19650 encourages appointing parties to define when information is needed in addition to what is needed.
Reference: ISO 19650-2, Clause 5.2.1 and 5.3.2 (Exchange Information Requirements and Delivery Milestones)

Information Delivery Milestone What We Need Why It Matters
Design Stage Expected performance, space requirements, standards Supports early decision-making, space planning, and regulatory approval
Procurement Model number, datasheets, lead times Ensures compatibility, delivery, and installation coordination
Installation Serial numbers, install date, installer name Builds the foundation for commissioning and warranty tracking
Commissioning Test results, commissioning date, certificates Validates performance, prepares for handover
Handover Full asset register, maintenance schedules, warranties Transfers responsibility to FM/operations team
Operation Maintenance logs, inspection records, replacement history Supports long-term planning, compliance, and audits
Replacement or Decommissioning End-of-life data, replacement options Aids capital planning and sustainability tracking
