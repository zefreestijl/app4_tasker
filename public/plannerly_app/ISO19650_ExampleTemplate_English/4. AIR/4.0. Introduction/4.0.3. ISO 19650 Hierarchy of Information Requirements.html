ISO 19650 Hierarchy of Information Requirements

To define our AIR clearly, it helps to understand how it fits into the overall information management process under the ISO 19650 framework.

ISO 19650 provides a structured way to define and manage information throughout the lifecycle of a built asset - from initial planning to operation. One of the core ideas is that not all information is created equal, different people need different types of information at different times, and for different purposes.
