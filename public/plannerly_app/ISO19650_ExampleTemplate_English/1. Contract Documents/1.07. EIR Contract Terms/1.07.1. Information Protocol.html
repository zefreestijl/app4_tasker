Information Protocol

The Information Protocol defines the contractual requirements for managing information in accordance with ISO 19650.

For this project, the Information Protocol is intended to be incorporated into future appointment documents following tender award. At this stage, the requirements listed below set the expectations that all tendering parties shall consider when preparing their responses.

The Information Protocol will define requirements for:

  • Interpretation
  • Co-ordination and resolution of conflicts
  • Obligations of the appointing party
  • Obligations of the appointee and appointor
  • CDE solution and workflow
  • Management of information
  • Level of information need
  • Use of information
  • Transfer of information
  • Liability
  • Remedies â€“ security
  • Termination
  • Protocol definitions
Why this matters

Defining Information Protocol expectations at the EIR stage ensures tendering parties understand the contractual approach to information management before appointment, while keeping detailed contractual clauses within the formal appointment documents issued post-award.
