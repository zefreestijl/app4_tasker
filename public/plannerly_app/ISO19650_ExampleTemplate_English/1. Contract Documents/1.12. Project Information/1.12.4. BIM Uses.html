Why this matters

The EIR defines the required BIM Uses and information outcomes. This BEP section turns those requirements into an executable plan by confirming what applies, assigning who delivers it, and checking readiness to proceed. This reduces ambiguity, improves accountability, and supports reliable delivery and verification.
