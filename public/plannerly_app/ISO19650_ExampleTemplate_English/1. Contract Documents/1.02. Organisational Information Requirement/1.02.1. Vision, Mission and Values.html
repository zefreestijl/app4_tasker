Our Vision, Mission and Values

We believe it is important to share the principles that guide how this office building will be planned, delivered, and operated.

These statements reflect the Ownerâ€™s priorities for long-term operation, asset performance, and effective information management. They provide context for why specific information is requested and how it will be used beyond project delivery.

Our vision

Supporting long-term building performance

  • Operate an office building that is efficient, safe, and easy to maintain throughout its lifecycle
  • Keep building information accurate, accessible, and usable for operational decision-making
  • Enable data-driven management of assets, spaces, and services
  • Reduce operational risk through clear, reliable, and structured information
Our mission

Turning information into operational value

  • Capture the right information at the right time to support maintenance, compliance, and asset management
  • Enable smooth handover from project delivery to building operation
  • Support facilities and asset managers with reliable asset and spatial data
  • Use structured digital information to improve efficiency, reduce rework, and control long-term costs
Our values

How information supports our operations

  • Information should be easy to understand, maintain, and use
  • Clear ownership of assets, data, and responsibilities
  • Standardised information that supports repeatable processes
  • Information that supports health, safety, and statutory obligations
  • Data that enables efficient operation and informed investment decisions

These principles inform the Organizational Information Requirements (OIR) and guide how information requirements are defined in the EIR.
