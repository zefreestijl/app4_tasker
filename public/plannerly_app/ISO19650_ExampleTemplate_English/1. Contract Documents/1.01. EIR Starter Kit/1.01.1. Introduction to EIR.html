What is an EIR?

The Exchange Information Requirements (EIR) sets out the Owner’s information needs for this project. It defines what information is required, why it’s needed, and how it will be delivered and used across the project lifecycle. It supports tendering, coordination, verification, and a successful handover to the Owner.

Who is this for?

  • Owners who need the right information for operations and handover
  • Consultants defining information requirements and delivery rules
  • Contractors responding to tender requirements and delivering structured outputs
