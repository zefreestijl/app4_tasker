Information standards, Information production methods and procedures

This section sets out the standards, methods, and procedures that apply to the production, exchange, and management of information on this project. All appointed parties shall comply with these requirements when creating, sharing, and delivering information.

2. Information production methods

The following information should be considered to support the work activities and contribute to the deliverables:

  • Project information and design information needed to enable construction
  • Alignment of the design with the needs of the operator, operations team or facility manager
  • Availability of a procedure or protocol for controlling the distribution of documents
  • Requirements to obtain firm price quotations for long-lead items
  • Updated lead times for major components and systems
  • Source of specialist maintenance service provision
  • Definition and extent of operation and maintenance information
  • Method statements for interfacing with existing utilities

5. Common Data Environment (CDE)

A Common Data Environment (CDE) shall be used to support the controlled sharing and management of information.

  • All information containers shall have a unique identifier.
  • Information status shall follow defined states (e.g. Work In Progress, Shared, Published).
  • Access shall be controlled based on role and responsibility.
