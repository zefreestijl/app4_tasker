Variations + Exclusions

Model Element Exclusions

  • Reinforcement, rebar detailing, and fabrication-level reinforcement models are excluded unless explicitly required for a defined BIM Use.
  • Temporary works, formwork, and construction sequencing models are excluded unless specifically instructed.

Element Size Thresholds

  • MEP elements below the agreed minimum size threshold (e.g. small bore pipework, minor containment, fixings) are not modelled unless required for coordination or safety-critical reasons.

Location-Based Exclusions

  • Tenant fit-out areas are excluded from the coordinated model unless otherwise stated.
  • Equipment inside proprietary vendor packages is excluded beyond defined connection points.

Information & Data Exclusions

  • Asset data outside the defined AIR (e.g. warranty data, maintenance schedules, serial numbers) is excluded unless explicitly requested.
  • COBie or structured asset data exports are excluded unless stated as a deliverable.

Coordination & Use Limitations

  • Models are not to be relied upon for setting-out, fabrication, or construction unless explicitly stated and approved.
  • Models are not contractual documents unless identified as such within the contract.
