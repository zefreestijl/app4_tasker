Specific Appointing Party/Owner Requirements

In addition to the Information Requirements added in the Scope module, here we include any specific BIM requirements stated by the Appointing Party/Owner.

It is important that the Appointing Party's requirements for BIM be considered early so that they can be incorporated into the projectâ€™s BIM processes. The completeness of these requirements will depend heavily on the Appointing Party's understanding of the benefits that BIM can bring.

[Image of SMART goals criteria specific measurable achievable relevant time-bound]

Our goal is to educate where necessary (in how BIM helps in the operation and maintenance of a building), ask targeted questions, and define SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound) requirements.

Here are some of the questions we will ask the Appointing Party:

Question Answer
What are the main challenges you experience when managing your buildings?
Have you used BIM on any projects in the past?
If yes, what have been some of the challenges you have experienced with BIM?
If yes, what have been the most successful experiences with BIM?
If no, can we share with you some of the major benefits for building owners?
