Hardware Specifications

The following hardware specifications are required to support the BIM uses defined in this plan.

BIM Role Image Specifications
Design Authoring
(BIM Manager / Modeler) 		The Stryker (or equivalent)
  • CPU: i9-9900K (Overclocked 4.9-5.3Ghz)
  • RAM: 32GB DDR4 (Min) / 64GB (Recommended)
  • GPU: NVIDIA RTX 2070 8GB (or newer RTX 40 series)
  • Storage: 512GB NVMe SSD (OS) + 1TB SSD (Data)
Model Access
(Project Managers) 		High-End Laptop (M-Series)
  • CPU: i9-9900K (Boost to 5.0Ghz)
  • RAM: 32GB DDR4
  • GPU: NVIDIA RTX 2070 8GB
  • Storage: 512GB NVMe SSD
Site Viewing
(Construction Foreman) 		iPad Pro (Wi-Fi + Cellular)
  • Storage: 128GB+ (for caching models)
  • Protection: UAG Rugged Case (or equivalent)
  • Connectivity: 5G/LTE for field data access
