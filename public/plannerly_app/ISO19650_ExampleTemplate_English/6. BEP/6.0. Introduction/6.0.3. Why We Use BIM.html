Why We Use BIM

The primary reasons we use Building Information Modeling (BIM) on our projects are to drive efficiency and value through the following objectives:

Key Objectives

  • Eliminating waste in design and construction processes.
  • Increasing productivity across all teams.
  • Improving design quality and reducing errors.
  • Reacting to industry demand.
  • Satisfying owner/appointing party requirements.
  • Gaining a competitive advantage.
  • Improving corporate innovation.
