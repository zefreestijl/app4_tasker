Task Team Definition

The Task Team is the most granular organizational unit within the project structure. It represents the specialized professionals responsible for the actual production of information and the physical execution of work.

Execution & Specialty

A person or group of people performing a specific task. Unlike the broader Delivery Team, a Task Team is focused on a specific discipline or package.

Example: The internal architectural design team, the structural engineering group, or a specialized curtain walling subcontractor.

BIM Manager Note: Each Task Team is responsible for maintaining its own Task Information Delivery Plan (TIDP), which details the specific deliverables and deadlines for their scope of work.
