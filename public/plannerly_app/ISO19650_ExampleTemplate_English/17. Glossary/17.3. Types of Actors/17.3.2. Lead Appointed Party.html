Lead Appointed Party Definition

The Lead Appointed Party is the primary entity within a Delivery Team. They serve as the critical interface between the technical production teams and the client (Appointing Party), ensuring that all information produced meets the project's quality and coordination standards.

Coordination & Accountability

The party who is accountable for coordinating information exchange between task teams or between a Delivery Team and the Appointing Party.

Strategic Output

  • The BIM Execution Plan (BEP)
  • The Master Information Delivery Plan (MIDP)
  • The Delivery Team Risk Register

Coordination Role

  • Cross-discipline coordination
  • Information Model Authorization
  • Task Team performance monitoring

Context for Taiwan Projects: This role is typically held by the Architect during the design phase and the Main Contractor during the construction phase.
